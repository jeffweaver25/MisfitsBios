Misfits Cluster Member Bios

This is where you can share and learn about your fellow clan/cluster mates.

There are two links you need to participate:

Submit a Bio
View Bios

Managing Bios - READ THIS!

Click Submit a Bio to create a bio. You can not use this link to update your bio (see below). This is a Google form — just fill it out.

When you submit your bio, be sure to save the URL of your form. You will need this if you want to update your bio later. (Click Edit your response and bookmark that page)" Don’t worry — if you lose it, DM FireJeff on Discord and I can send it to you.

Similarly, if you want to delete your bio, just DM FireJeff to have it deleted.

Images (READ THIS!)

You can upload up to 3 images, but you must be logged in to your Google account to submit images.

You can upload up to 3 images, but you must be logged in to your Google account to submit images.
Warning! If someone views your image, they will see the name on your Google account.

Viewing Bios

Use the View Bios link to view bios that have been submitted.

You can filter by Discord or in-game usernames (or a portion of the name). Enter the name and press “Go.” The search is case insensitive. You can further filter your search by selecting a specific clan or role.

To see uploaded images, click on the image thumbnails — they will open full-size in a new tab.
